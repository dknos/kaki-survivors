2. Line weight

One pen. One weight per role. Stop using brush size as a stylistic variable.

Primary outline · 4 px
Secondary detail · 2 px
Whisker / hint · 1 px

Reference is at 1024-tall canvas. Scale linearly for other resolutions.

3. Shadow + highlight (single angle, flat)

Light from upper-left. Shadow falls lower-right. Flat — no gradients.

Round form — single oval highlight + lower-right shadow facet.
Flat form — single facet shadow on facing side + thin highlight band.
Pointed form — same upper-left highlight, same lower-right shadow side.

4. Perspective & framing

Game uses two cameras. Brief each asset against the right one.

A. Horde view
Orthographic top-down, ~60° tilt. Read by silhouette + palette. Faces secondary.
B. House interior
Orthographic iso, ~30° tilt. 3/4 face matters. Slight foreshortening, never extreme.

Flat-shaded 3D meshes with hand-drawn 2D decals layered on top. You will mostly be drawing the 2D decals; the dev handles the 3D base.

7. First-wave commission queue

  1. Paper-grain texture (Micro) — shared overlay; everyone draws against this.
  2. Kitty Kaki idle/interaction sheet (Small) — front + 3/4 + back stances.
  3. House key art (Room) — anchor visual all subsequent rooms reference.
  4. 12 reusable prop decals (Micro × 3) — yarn ball, teacup, sketchbook, stamp, paper plane, inkblot, framed sketch, postal stamp, blossom branch, tea kettle, ribbon spool, paw print.
  5. 6 minigame icons (Micro) — one per shipped minigame.

8. What NOT to do

  • ❌ Photoreal rendering, soft brushes, painted-volumetric lighting.
  • ❌ Off-palette pastel washes "just to add variety."
  • ❌ AI-generated assets without heavy redraw on top.
  • ❌ Five different line weights on one character.
  • ❌ Tilted/dutch-angle props that won't sit on the iso floor grid.
  • ❌ Pure black (#000) or pure white (#fff). Always Ink and Highlight.
  • ❌ Adding ambient occlusion bake — the engine handles AO.
  • ❌ Vibe briefs. Always cite spec + asset name + dimensions.

9. Filenames & delivery

One zip per commission. Filename pattern:

kk_<category>_<asset>_<variant>.png — e.g. kk_prop_teacup_steaming.png, kk_room_kitchen_anchor.png.

Include both 1024 master and 512 game-ready resize. Source PSD/Procreate/Krita optional but appreciated. Drop a .txt noting which palette slots were used; that lets us cross-reference style drift later.
